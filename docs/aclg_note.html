Appendix A — A note on the Australian Classification of Local Government

Author

Sreehari Pulickamadhom Sreedhar

Published

September 16, 2025

The Australian Classification of Local Governments (ACLG) was a system maintained and published by the Department of Infrastructure, Transport, Regional Development, Communications, Sport and the Arts (DITRDCSA) (DITRDCSA 2022). The Department is aiming to phase out the ACLG system in favour of the ABS Australian Statistical Geographical System Remoteness classifcation.
